UK / Great Britain / England

Key difference

The United Kingdom / UK = England+Scotland+Wales+Northern Ireland
Great Britain = England+Scotland+Wales
England = one nation inside the UK, not the whole UK

Test trap

Northern Ireland

Northern Ireland is part of the UK, but it is not part of Great Britain. This is one of the most common Country Study traps.

Remember: Great Britain does not include Northern Ireland.
